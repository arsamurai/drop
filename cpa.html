Як працює арбітраж

  • 1
    Пункт 1

    Look for installers with professional certifications such as NABCEP (North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners). Check their experience and track record in the solar industry.

  • Пункт 2

    Choose an installer who takes the time to assess your specific energy needs and property characteristics. They should provide personalised recommendations and design a system that optimises your energy production and savings.

    2
  • 3
    Пункт 3

    Obtain detailed quotes from multiple installers to compare pricing and value. Consider the long-term return on investment, not just the upfront cost. Inquire about available financing options, such as loans, leases, or power purchase agreements (PPAs).

  • Пункт 4

    Read online reviews and testimonials from previous customers. Ask for references and contact them to gather firsthand experiences.

    4
  • 5
    Пункт 5

    Inquire about the quality and brands of solar panels, inverters, and mounting systems they use. Ask about their installation process and the workmanship warranties they offer. Review examples of their previous installations and case studies.

  • Финальный пункт

    Choose an installer who takes the time to assess your specific energy needs and property characteristics. They should provide personalised recommendations and design a system that optimises your energy production and savings.
